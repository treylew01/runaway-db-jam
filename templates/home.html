Breaking free is a database that is being created to tell the stories of enslaved individuals living in a slave society

Runaway ads are ads that enslavers would post in newspapers to locate fugitives slaves that had runaway from there place enslavement

These ads offer a glimpse of what life was like for people living in a slave society

Escape or "Marronage" was a common form of resistance to the hardships of slavery Carribean Marronage was different as there were only such limited places for people to run to on an island. Most marronage attempts would not be successful and these people would most certianly be punished harshly

This database is created to tell the stories of people

Outside of the typical information these ads would held such as enslaver, poster, suspected, runaway area, etc. These ads reveal alot about the people. The scars and branding of enslavement, prescribed ethnicity and name, the languages they spoke, and their intentions

A database by you for you

Part of creating this website is to remove the barrier of access to this information that many people who don't study traditional academia would face. Creating this open source database would allow academics, students, and the people of the Carribean learn more about their past.
